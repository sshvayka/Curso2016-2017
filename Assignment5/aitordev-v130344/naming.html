Naming strategy

  • Domain: http://semanticweb.org/

  • Ontological terms path: http://semanticweb.org/onto#

  • Individual path: http://semanticweb.org/resources/

  • Ontological terms pattern: http://semanticweb.org/onto#

  • Individual pattern: http://semanticweb.org/resources/<resource_type>/<resource_name>
