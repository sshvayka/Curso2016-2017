Define Resource Naming Strategy

Hash URIs for ontological terms, slash URIs for individuals

  • Domain: http://datos.madrid.es/ Ontological terms path: http://www.semanticweb.org/group01#
  • Individuals path: http://www.semanticweb.org/group01/individuals
  • Ontological terms pattern: http://www.semanticweb.org/group01#<term_name>
  • Individuals pattern: http://www.semanticweb.org/group01/resource/<resource_type>/<resource_name>
