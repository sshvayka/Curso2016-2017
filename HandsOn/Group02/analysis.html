Analyse Licensing of the Data Source

-Publisher & Rightholder

The publisher for the bus stop CSV is Transport for London (TfL).
The publisher & the rightsholder for the Underground CSV is Chris Bell(The data is published in his/her personal website).

-License

The license for the bus stop CSV is UK Open Government Licence (OGL v2).
Please see link to TfL website for Terms & Conditions related to this dataset.
Direct LINK to the Terms & Conditions.

The license for the underground CSV is the specified by Chris Bell in the website.
As it's said in the licensing, we can use this CSV under a public domain license because there isn't any other comment or copyright notice saying that the CSV comes from another source.

Contains public sector information licensed under the Open Government Licence v2.0.
