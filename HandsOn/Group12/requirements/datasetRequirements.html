Dataset requirements

  • Data are in the smart cities domain. They come from Ayuntamiento de Madrid.
  • Data are available as CSV file. Yes, they are.
  • Data have an open license so it can be published.
  • Data can be easily linked with generic real-world entities. They can be linked with Districts (distritos) and Neighbours (barrios)
  • Documentation exists for the data. Yes. An explanation of every field is given.
