Application Requirements

Description

The application will use the dataset provided by datos.madrid.es to show which streets or zones are the most affected by any kind of citizen announcement (city breakdowns, malfunciton of bicimad and some others warnings related to the city). Also, It will represent the efectiveness of the services involved (such as firefighters, maintenance of bicimad, clean services, etc), as well as, some graphical statisitcs of breakdowns and announcements around the city.
